<size=50>Notes When Editing Puzzle Sets</size>

1. Please ensure the questions' accuracy and interest, every question should have explanations.
2. It should be 30 to 100 questions in a set, please double check before upload.
3. Please use written language to minimize typos.
4. Please not involving sensitive or controversial topics.
5. DO NOT copy from other's puzzle set.
6. Try to avoid the problem of timeliness.
