<size=50><color=#4FF0FFFF>How to play</color></size>

Mouse Control: LButton Confirm, RButton Cancel/Return.

Keyboard: Up Down Left Right to focus, Enter to select
1 2 3 4 equals to A B C D

Rules:
◆ All questions have only one answer.
◆ Energy reduced by 1 if answered wrong, and increased 1 if right.
◆ You can continue answering after you answered wrong, but you fail when energy falls to 0.
◆ Every round has 10 questions, the question will not appear in the following rounds if your first answer is right, and vice versa.
◆ Once you finished all 100 questions in a package, you will unlock an illustration.
